# **AWS Cloud GRC & ISO 27001 Audit Simulation**

**Objective:** To design a simulated AWS cloud environment and conduct a formal Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) audit based on ISO 27001:2022 and UK GDPR standards.

**Scenario:** Acted as the lead GRC Analyst for a fictional high-security firm (Tojo Clan UK Ltd.) to evaluate their cloud infrastructure, map existing controls, and generate an enterprise risk register to present to executive leadership.

**Skills & Tools Demonstrated:**

- Cloud Infrastructure Auditing (AWS IAM, S3, CloudTrail)

- ISO 27001:2022 Control Mapping

- UK GDPR Data Protection Assessment

- Risk Identification, Scoring, and Mitigation (Risk Register generation)

- Principle of Least Privilege & RBAC Evaluation

**Repository Contents:**

1. **Architecture & Audit Report:** Details the AWS environment setup and the initial ISO 27001 gap analysis.

2. **Enterprise Risk Register:** A formal risk matrix scoring identified vulnerabilities (e.g., exposed PII in S3) and proposing remediation strategies.
